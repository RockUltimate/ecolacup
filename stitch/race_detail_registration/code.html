Entry Requirements

The High Sierra is as unforgiving as it is beautiful. To maintain the prestige and safety of the Heritage Field series, all participants must meet the following criteria:

history_edu

Prior Experience

Proof of completion of at least one 50K trail race with 5,000ft+ gain within the last 24 months.
